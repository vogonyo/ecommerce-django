Multilab Systems Kenya Limited is a private company incorporated in Kenya in 2015. It was established to supply essential laboratory equipment, instruments, apparatus, chemicals, reagents, diagnostic kits, personal safety gear and consumables required by research, diagnostic, analytical laboratories, learning institutions and school based laboratories. Supplies are sourced from reputable manufacturing companies across the globe.

Our vision is to be a reference Centre for Biomedical Laboratory supply solutions

Our mission is to be a dependable partner in laboratory supplies and related services You order we supply. For items not in the country already we make arrangements to specifically import them for confirmed orders.
